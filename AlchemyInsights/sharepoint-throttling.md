# <a name="sharepoint-online-throttling"></a>SharePoint Online 節流

SharePoint Online 使用節流以維持最佳的效能與可靠性的 SharePoint Online 服務。 節流限制的使用者動作或並行數目 （來呼叫指令碼或程式碼） 以避免過度使用的資源。 如需詳細資訊，請瀏覽下列連結。

- [資料移轉和 SPO 節流](https://blogs.technet.microsoft.com/sposupport/2017/08/12/data-migration-and-spo-service-throttling/)

- [SharePoint Online 和 OneDrive 的移轉速度](https://docs.microsoft.com/sharepointmigration/sharepoint-online-and-onedrive-migration-speed)

 - [處理 SharePoint Online 使用指數回關閉節流設定](https://docs.microsoft.com/sharepoint/dev/solution-guidance/handle-sharepoint-online-throttling-by-using-exponential-back-off)

- [容量規劃和負載測試 SharePoint Online](https://support.office.com/article/Capacity-planning-and-load-testing-SharePoint-Online-c932bd9b-fb9a-47ab-a330-6979d03688c0)
