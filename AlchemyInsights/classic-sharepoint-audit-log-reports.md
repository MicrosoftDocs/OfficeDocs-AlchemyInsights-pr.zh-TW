# <a name="classic-sharepoint-audit-log-reports"></a>傳統的 SharePoint audit 記錄報告

- SharePoint Online 不支援**內容查看**審查記錄報告。 當您嘗試儲存報告時, 您會收到錯誤:**報告不包含任何資料**。

- 您必須使用安全性 & 合規性中心內的「審核記錄搜尋」功能, 才能取得 SharePoint 和 OneDrive 相關的審計記錄檔。 如需詳細資訊, 請參閱[Search the audit log](https://docs.microsoft.com/office365/securitycompliance/search-the-audit-log-in-security-and-compliance#search-the-audit-log)。

- SharePoint 和 OneDrive 預設支援下列已審核的活動: 檔案[和頁面活動](https://docs.microsoft.com/office365/securitycompliance/search-the-audit-log-in-security-and-compliance#file-and-page-activities)、[資料夾活動](https://docs.microsoft.com/office365/securitycompliance/search-the-audit-log-in-security-and-compliance#folder-activities)、[共用和存取要求活動](https://docs.microsoft.com/office365/securitycompliance/search-the-audit-log-in-security-and-compliance#sharing-and-access-request-activities)、[同步處理活動](https://docs.microsoft.com/office365/securitycompliance/search-the-audit-log-in-security-and-compliance#synchronization-activities), 以及[網站管理活動](https://docs.microsoft.com/office365/securitycompliance/search-the-audit-log-in-security-and-compliance#site-administration-activities)。
