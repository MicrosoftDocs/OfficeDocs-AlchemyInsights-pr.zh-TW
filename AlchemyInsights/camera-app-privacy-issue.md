# <a name="camera-app-privacy-settings"></a><span data-ttu-id="10092-102">相機應用程式隱私權設定</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="10092-102">Camera App Privacy Settings</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="10092-103">您的隱私權設定似乎正在阻止應用程式存取您的相機。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="10092-103">It sounds like your privacy settings are stopping the app from accessing your camera.</span></span>

1.  <span data-ttu-id="10092-104">從 [**開始**] 選擇 [**設定**] (齒輪形狀圖示) > [**隱私權**]  >  [**相機**]。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="10092-104">From **Start**, choose **Settings** (Gear-shaped icon) > **Privacy** > **Camera**.</span></span>

2.  <span data-ttu-id="10092-105">在 [**允許存取這個裝置上的相機**] 底下，選取 [**變更**]。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="10092-105">Under **Allow access to the camera on this device**, select **Change**.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="10092-106">確定切換開關已經是 [**開啟**]。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="10092-106">Make sure the toggle is turned **On**.</span></span>

3.  <span data-ttu-id="10092-107">接下來，在 [**允許應用程式存取您的相機**] 底下，確定切換開關已經是 [**開啟**]。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="10092-107">Next, under **Allow apps to access your camera**, make sure the toggle is turned **On**.</span></span>

4.  <span data-ttu-id="10092-108">然後，在 [**選擇哪些應用程式可以存取您的相機**] 底下，確定您想要的應用程式旁邊的切換開關也是 [**開啟**]。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="10092-108">Then, under **Choose which apps can access your camera**, make sure that the toggle next to your desired app is turned **On** as well.</span></span>

5.  <span data-ttu-id="10092-109">如果您在清單中找不到您嘗試使用的應用程式，或者如果您是從網站或光碟安裝應用程式，則可能是桌面應用程式。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="10092-109">If you can't find the app you are trying to use in the list, or if you installed the app from a website or disc, it is likely a desktop app.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="10092-110">若要啟用桌面應用程式的相機存取權，請開啟 [**允許桌面應用程式存取您的相機**]。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="10092-110">To enable camera access for desktop apps, turn on **Allow desktop apps to access your camera**.</span></span>
