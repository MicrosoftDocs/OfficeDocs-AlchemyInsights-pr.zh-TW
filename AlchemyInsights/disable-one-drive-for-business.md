# <a name="disable-onedrive-for-business"></a>停用商務 OneDrive

您可以使用數種方式視您的業務需求停用 OneDrive 和其服務：

- [關閉、停用或卸載 OneDrive](https://support.office.com/article/turn-off-disable-or-uninstall-onedrive-f32a17ce-3336-40fe-9c38-6efb09f944b0)
- [只允許同步處理特定組織的 OneDrive 帳戶](https://docs.microsoft.com/onedrive/use-group-policy#allow-syncing-onedrive-accounts-for-only-specific-organizations)
- [停用某些使用者的 OneDrive 網站建立](https://docs.microsoft.com/sharepoint/manage-user-profiles#disable-onedrive-creation-for-some-users)
- [根據網路位置，控制 SharePoint 線上及 OneDrive 資料的存取權](https://docs.microsoft.com/sharepoint/control-access-based-on-network-location)
- [防止使用者同步個人 OneDrive 帳戶](https://docs.microsoft.com/onedrive/use-group-policy#DisablePersonalSync)
- [管理 Windows 裝置與 OneDrive 及其他服務之間的連線](https://docs.microsoft.com/windows/privacy/manage-connections-from-windows-operating-system-components-to-microsoft-services#bkmk-onedrive)
