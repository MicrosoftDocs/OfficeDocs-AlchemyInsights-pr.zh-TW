# <a name="disable-onedrive-for-business"></a>停用商務用 OneDrive

有幾種方式，您可以根據您的業務需求停用 OneDrive 與其服務存取權：

- [關閉、 停用或解除安裝 OneDrive](https://support.office.com/article/turn-off-disable-or-uninstall-onedrive-f32a17ce-3336-40fe-9c38-6efb09f944b0)
- [僅針對特定組織允許 OneDrive 帳戶進行同步處理](https://docs.microsoft.com/onedrive/use-group-policy#allow-syncing-onedrive-accounts-for-only-specific-organizations)
- [停用某些使用者的 OneDrive 網站架設](https://docs.microsoft.com/sharepoint/manage-user-profiles#disable-onedrive-creation-for-some-users)
- [SharePoint Online 和 OneDrive 資料是根據網路位置控制存取](https://docs.microsoft.com/sharepoint/control-access-based-on-network-location)
- [防止使用者同步處理個人 OneDrive 帳戶](https://docs.microsoft.com/onedrive/use-group-policy#DisablePersonalSync)
- [從 Windows 裝置管理連線至 OneDrive 和其他服務](https://docs.microsoft.com/windows/privacy/manage-connections-from-windows-operating-system-components-to-microsoft-services#bkmk-onedrive)
