# <a name="add-update-or-remove-your-payment-methods"></a>新增、更新或移除付款方式

您可以使用**信用卡、轉帳卡或銀行帳戶**購買 Microsoft 的產品和服務。 您可以在 [[付款方式](https://go.microsoft.com/fwlink/p/?linkid=2018806)] 頁面或進行購買時，輸入您的付款資訊。

- [新增付款方式](https://docs.microsoft.com/microsoft-365/commerce/billing-and-payments/manage-payment-methods#add-a-payment-method)

- [更新付款方式詳細資料](https://docs.microsoft.com/microsoft-365/commerce/billing-and-payments/manage-payment-methods#update-payment-method-details)

- [取代付款方式](https://docs.microsoft.com/microsoft-365/commerce/billing-and-payments/manage-payment-methods#replace-a-payment-method)

- [刪除付款方式](https://docs.microsoft.com/microsoft-365/commerce/billing-and-payments/manage-payment-methods#delete-a-payment-method)

- [針對付款方式進行疑難排解](https://docs.microsoft.com/microsoft-365/commerce/billing-and-payments/manage-payment-methods#troubleshoot-payment-methods)
