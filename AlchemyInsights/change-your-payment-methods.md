# <a name="add-update-or-remove-your-payment-methods"></a>新增、更新或移除付款方式

您可以使用**信用卡、轉帳卡或銀行帳戶**購買 Microsoft 的產品和服務。 您可以在 [[付款方式](https://go.microsoft.com/fwlink/p/?linkid=2018806)] 頁面或進行購買時，輸入您的付款資訊。

- [新增付款方式](https://docs.microsoft.com/microsoft-365/commerce/billing-and-payments/add-update-or-remove-credit-card-or-bank-account?view=o365-worldwide#add-a-payment-method)

- [更新現有的付款方式](https://docs.microsoft.com/microsoft-365/commerce/billing-and-payments/add-update-or-remove-credit-card-or-bank-account?view=o365-worldwide#update-an-existing-payment-method)

- [變更付款方式](https://docs.microsoft.com/microsoft-365/commerce/billing-and-payments/add-update-or-remove-credit-card-or-bank-account?view=o365-worldwide#change-a-payment-method)

- [移除付款方式](https://docs.microsoft.com/microsoft-365/commerce/billing-and-payments/add-update-or-remove-credit-card-or-bank-account?view=o365-worldwide#remove-a-payment-method)

- [針對付款方式進行疑難排解](https://docs.microsoft.com/microsoft-365/commerce/billing-and-payments/add-update-or-remove-credit-card-or-bank-account?view=o365-worldwide#troubleshoot-payment-methods)
