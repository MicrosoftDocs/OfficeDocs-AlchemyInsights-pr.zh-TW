# <a name="office-apps-crash-stop-responding-or-dont-launch"></a>Office 應用程式損毀，停止回應，或不會啟動

若要修正 Office 應用程式停止回應或當機，請嘗試下列選項：

- [調查可能的問題增益集](https://support.office.com/article/powerpoint-isn-t-responding-hangs-or-freezes-652ede6e-e3d2-449a-a07f-8c800dfb948d#bkmk_addins)的 Office 安裝
- 檢查是否與 Office 應用程式的 [[防毒軟體的衝突](https://support.office.com/article/powerpoint-isn-t-responding-hangs-or-freezes-652ede6e-e3d2-449a-a07f-8c800dfb948d?ocmsassetID#bkmk_conflict)
- 確認或[安裝 Office 的最新的更新](https://support.office.com/article/update-office-and-your-computer-with-microsoft-update-2ab296f3-7f03-43a2-8e50-46de917611c5)
- 使用控制台[修復 Office](https://support.office.com/article/repair-an-office-application-7821d4b6-7c1d-4205-aa0e-a6b40c5bb88b)
- [解除安裝](https://support.office.com/article/uninstall-office-from-a-pc-9dd49b83-264a-477a-8fcc-2fdf5dbf61d8)再重新安裝 Office

如需詳細資訊，請參閱：
- [沒有回應，Excel 停止回應，會凍結或停止運作](https://support.office.com/article/excel-not-responding-hangs-freezes-or-stops-working-37e7d3c9-9e84-40bf-a805-4ca6853a1ff4)
- [PowerPoint 未回應，停止回應或凍結](https://support.office.com/article/powerpoint-isn-t-responding-hangs-or-freezes-652ede6e-e3d2-449a-a07f-8c800dfb948d)
- [在 [我的電腦上啟動 Office 應用程式時出現 「 停止運作 」 錯誤](https://support.office.com/article/i-get-a-stopped-working-error-when-i-start-office-applications-on-my-pc-52bd7985-4e99-4a35-84c8-2d9b8301a2fa)
- [協助保護您的檔案時損毀](https://support.office.com/article/help-protect-your-files-in-case-of-a-crash-551c29b1-6a4b-4415-a3ff-a80415b92f99)
