# <a name="how-do-i-change-from-credit-card-payments-to-invoice"></a><span data-ttu-id="a770f-102">如何從信用卡付款變更為發票？</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a770f-102">How do I change from credit card payments to invoice?</span></span>

> [!NOTE]
> <span data-ttu-id="a770f-p101">只有在您的 Office 365 訂閱成本超過特定金額 (依服務位置而異) 時，才能使用以發票付款的選項。較大金額的發票付款可能會需要信用查核。若要深入了解，請參閱[支付商務用 Office 365 的訂閱費用](https://support.office.com/article/734f4aab-df2d-4e9b-8cb1-691910bde216)。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a770f-p101">The option to pay by invoice is only available if your Office 365 subscription costs over a certain amount (which varies by service location). A credit check might be required for larger invoice payments. To learn more, see [Pay for your Office 365 for business subscription](https://support.office.com/article/734f4aab-df2d-4e9b-8cb1-691910bde216).</span></span>
  
1. <span data-ttu-id="a770f-106">在系統管理中心中, 移至 [[訂閱](https://go.microsoft.com/fwlink/p/?linkid=842054)] 頁面, 或選擇 [**帳單** \> **訂閱**]。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a770f-106">In the Admin Center, go to the [Subscriptions](https://go.microsoft.com/fwlink/p/?linkid=842054) page, or choose **Billing** \> **Subscriptions**.</span></span>

2. <span data-ttu-id="a770f-107">選取您要變更的訂閱。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a770f-107">Choose the subscription that you want to change.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="a770f-108">在 [**付款條件**] 區段中, 選擇 [**變更付款明細**]。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a770f-108">In the **Payment method** section, choose **Change payment details**.</span></span>

3. <span data-ttu-id="a770f-109">在 [**變更付款明細**] 窗格中, 選擇 [**變更您的付款條件**]。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a770f-109">In the **Change payment details** pane, choose **Change your payment method**.</span></span>
<br><span data-ttu-id="a770f-110">*附注: 如果未顯示 [**變更您的付款條件**] 連結, 表示從信用卡或銀行帳戶切換為 [發票] 無法使用該訂閱, 因為訂閱不符合計費的最低成本金額。發票計費所需的最低成本金額會依地理位置而異。*</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a770f-110">*NOTE: If the **Change your payment method** link isn't displayed, it means that switching from credit card or bank account to invoice isn't available for the subscription because the subscription doesn't meet the minimum cost amount for billing by invoice. The minimum cost amount required to be billed by invoice varies by geographic location.*</span></span>
  
4. <span data-ttu-id="a770f-111">在 [**變更您的付款條件**] 對話方塊中, 選擇 [**繼續**]。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a770f-111">In the **Change your payment method** dialog box, choose **Continue**.</span></span>
