# <a name="how-do-i-change-from-credit-card-payments-to-invoice"></a><span data-ttu-id="8005d-102">如何變更從信用卡付款發票？</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="8005d-102">How do I change from credit card payments to invoice?</span></span>

> [!NOTE]
> <span data-ttu-id="8005d-p101">只有在您的 Office 365 訂閱成本超過特定金額 (依服務位置而異) 時，才能使用以發票付款的選項。較大金額的發票付款可能會需要信用查核。若要深入了解，請參閱[支付商務用 Office 365 的訂閱費用](https://support.office.com/article/734f4aab-df2d-4e9b-8cb1-691910bde216)。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="8005d-p101">The option to pay by invoice is only available if your Office 365 subscription costs over a certain amount (which varies by service location). A credit check might be required for larger invoice payments. To learn more, see [Pay for your Office 365 for business subscription](https://support.office.com/article/734f4aab-df2d-4e9b-8cb1-691910bde216).</span></span> 
  
1. <span data-ttu-id="8005d-106">在管理中心中，移至 [[訂閱](https://go.microsoft.com/fwlink/p/?linkid=842054)] 頁面或選擇 [**計費** \> **訂閱**。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="8005d-106">In the Admin Center, go to the [Subscriptions](https://go.microsoft.com/fwlink/p/?linkid=842054) page, or choose **Billing** \> **Subscriptions**.</span></span>
    
2. <span data-ttu-id="8005d-p102">選擇您要變更的訂閱。[**付費方式**] 區段中選擇 [**變更付款詳細資料**]。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="8005d-p102">Choose the subscription that you want to change. In the **Payment method** section, choose **Change payment details**.</span></span>
    
3. <span data-ttu-id="8005d-109">在 [**變更付款詳細資料**] 窗格中，選擇 [**變更付款方法**。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="8005d-109">In the **Change payment details** pane, choose **Change your payment method**.</span></span>
<br><span data-ttu-id="8005d-110">*附註： 如果也不會顯示 [**變更您的付費方式**] 連結，就表示從信用卡或銀行帳戶發票切換不適用於訂閱因為訂閱不會以發票達成帳務最低成本量。付費給發票所需的最低成本量會依地理位置而異。*</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="8005d-110">*NOTE: If the **Change your payment method** link isn't displayed, it means that switching from credit card or bank account to invoice isn't available for the subscription because the subscription doesn't meet the minimum cost amount for billing by invoice. The minimum cost amount required to be billed by invoice varies by geographic location.*</span></span>
  
4. <span data-ttu-id="8005d-111">在 [\* \* 變更您的付費方式 \* \*] 對話方塊] 方塊中，選擇 [**繼續]**。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="8005d-111">In the \*\* Change your payment method \*\* dialog box, choose **Continue**.</span></span>
    
5. <span data-ttu-id="8005d-112">選擇 **[確定]** 回到管理中心。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="8005d-112">Choose **OK** to return to the Admin center.</span></span>
