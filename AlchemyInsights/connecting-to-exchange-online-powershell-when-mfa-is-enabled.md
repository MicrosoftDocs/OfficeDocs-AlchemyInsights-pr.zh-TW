# <a name="connect-to-exchange-online-powershell-when-mfa-is-enabled"></a>啟用 MFA 時連線至 Exchange Online PowerShell

如果您的帳戶已啟用多重要素驗證 (MFA), 您必須遵循這些指示來連線至 Exchange Online PowerShell:[使用多重要素驗證連線至 Exchange Online powershell](https://docs.microsoft.com/powershell/exchange/exchange-online/connect-to-exchange-online-powershell/mfa-connect-to-exchange-online-powershell)。

**附注**: 即使您曾經使用[一般連線指示](https://docs.microsoft.com/powershell/exchange/exchange-online/connect-to-exchange-online-powershell/connect-to-exchange-online-powershell)連線至 Exchange Online PowerShell, 在為您的帳戶啟用 mfa 之後, 您必須使用 mfa 連線指示。
